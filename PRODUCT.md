# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Redline Academy students, trainers, administrators, and marketers use this site to move between public enrollment, course delivery, schedules, assessments, payments, content management, and reporting. Dashboard users are usually trying to complete an operational task quickly: continue learning, review course material, manage course content, create events, grade work, or monitor progress.

## Product Purpose

Redline Academy is a bilingual caregiver training website and role-based LMS. It supports public program discovery and Supabase-backed learning operations for students, trainers, admins, and marketers. Success means each role can understand their current state, find the next action, and complete routine LMS work without confusion or unnecessary navigation.

## Brand Personality

Professional, practical, and trustworthy. The product should feel calm and credible for vocational training operations, with enough polish to reflect an internationally oriented education brand.

## Anti-references

Avoid generic SaaS decoration, dashboard card clutter, marketing-style hero sections inside authenticated tools, overly playful controls, low-contrast gray text, and layouts that collapse poorly on mobile.

## Design Principles

- Lead with the working surface and the next useful action.
- Keep role-specific workflows dense enough for repeated use, but visually ordered.
- Preserve existing behavior and copy unless a verified UI problem requires a change.
- Use consistent dashboard components across student, trainer, and admin surfaces.
- Make mobile layouts structural, not just squeezed desktop views.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Default to accessible contrast, keyboard-operable dashboard controls, visible focus states, reduced-motion-safe transitions, and responsive layouts that keep course, schedule, and form content readable on small screens.
