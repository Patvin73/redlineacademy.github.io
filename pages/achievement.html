Who We Are

ABOUT THE PROGRAM

🎯

The Mission

Redline Academy is a joint initiative to deliver industry-recognised caregiving micro-credentials to communities in Eastern Indonesia — starting with Ambon and expanding across Maluku province.

🏥

The Focus

Care for the elderly and people with disabilities. Graduates are equipped for roles in aged care homes, hospitals, clinics, community outreach, and in-home service environments.

📡

The Method

Fully online LMS-based delivery with Zoom live sessions, psychometric screening, structured assessment tasks, and community volunteer placement — accessible from any island in Maluku.

📺

Media Presence

Featured on TVRI Maluku in a live broadcast interview — validating the program's credibility and public-facing reach across the province.

🏫

School Partnerships

Engaged SMK 11 (Central Maluku / Seram Island) and SMK 6 Ambon, with formal requests to deliver our Short & Full Course in-school programs to vocational students.

🌏

Regional Expansion

Market socialisation conducted across Sparua Island, Seram Island (West Seram), and Buru Island — with targeted outreach to nursing homes and community health centres.

Curriculum

PILOT COMPETENCY UNITS

01

Communication

Effective verbal and non-verbal communication skills for caregiving contexts — building trust, delivering clear instructions, and documenting care outcomes. Delivered online with supporting video content and practice scenarios.

02

Empowerment

Supporting client autonomy, dignity, and independence. Participants learn person-centred care principles, advocacy skills, and how to promote active participation in daily care decisions for elderly and disabled clients.

03

Hygiene & Infection Control

Comprehensive infection prevention and hygiene management protocols — including respiratory hygiene, personal care standards, and evidence-based infection control applicable to community and residential care settings.

Delivery Method

ONLINE LEARNING INFRASTRUCTURE

All units delivered fully online via LMS platform with video content hosted via YouTube. Live interaction conducted through Zoom, enabling real-time assessor-student engagement across remote islands. Canva-designed presentation decks support visual learning objectives.

Our Capacity

COMPANY CAPABILITY

🖥️

Online Learning Delivery

Full LMS capability including course setup, content sequencing, video module hosting, and learner tracking — proven through Cohort 1 delivery across remote Indonesian islands.

📊

Psychometric Assessment

End-to-end online psychometric screening using certified assessors via Zoom. Multi-dimensional profiling across IQ, personality, emotional competency, vocational aptitude and thinking ability.

🎓

Skills-Set Certification

Structured micro-credentials aligned to industry competency standards. Three core units delivered in Pilot 1: Communication, Empowerment, and Hygiene & Infection Control.

🤝

Institutional Partnerships

Active engagement with vocational schools (SMK 6, SMK 11), health facilities, nursing homes, and government bodies — demonstrating real-world placement and community integration capacity.

📡

Remote Geographic Reach

Program successfully delivered and marketed across multiple islands including Ambon, Seram, Buru, and Sparua — demonstrating scalable remote learning infrastructure.

📣

Public Communications

Media presence established through TVRI Maluku live broadcast. Social media activation across Instagram, Facebook, and Twitter/X with bilingual (English/Indonesian) content strategy.

🌱

Community Outreach

Volunteer program framework developed for elderly care homes (Panti Jompo). Field socialisation conducted with community health centres to build trust and create real-world practice pathways.

📈

Scalable Program Model

Short course and full course pathways designed for rapid expansion. MoU templates prepared for health facility partnerships. Government recognition pathway initiated with Ambon Mayor's office.
