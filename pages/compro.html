Who We Are

Purpose-Built
for Care
Professionals

Redline Academy is an Australian-registered vocational education provider specialising in caregiver training that meets Australian Micro-credentials Framework (AUS NMF) standards. We are purpose-built to serve Indonesian caregiving talent — delivering world-class credentials at local price points.

Founded to close the gap between global demand for qualified care workers and the limited access to internationally recognised training in emerging markets, Redline Academy offers a complete career pathway: from caring for a family member on Day One, through to professional employment in Australia, the United Kingdom, Canada, and Singapore.

Our programmes are delivered entirely online, making professional certification accessible regardless of geography, income bracket, or prior experience in the health sector.

To make world-standard caregiver education accessible to every Indonesian who wants to serve with dignity — locally today, globally when ready.

— Mission Statement, Redline Academy

Our Difference

Why Redline Academy

Australian-Standard Credentials

Our certifications are issued under the Australian Micro-credentials Framework — not generic local certificates. This distinction is recognised and valued by healthcare employers across four international markets.

Accessible Pricing for Indonesian Learners

World-class training starting from IDR 500,000 per month breaks down the financial barrier that has historically excluded Indonesian caregivers from globally competitive education.

Immediate, Practical Value

Unlike programmes that only serve those seeking overseas employment, Redline graduates can apply their skills from Day One — at home, locally, and globally as their career develops.

Fully Online & Flexible

Designed for working adults and family carers, our 15-week curriculum is delivered entirely online — accessible from anywhere in Indonesia without disrupting existing responsibilities.

Dedicated, Expert Educators

Our trainers are deeply specialised in aged care, disability support, and person-centred practice. They bring both clinical expertise and a genuine passion for learner success.

A Global Vision with Local Roots

Redline Academy is built on the conviction that Indonesian care workers deserve access to the same quality of education as their counterparts in developed nations — and that this investment benefits individuals, families, and the global healthcare system.
